Privacy Policy for Pitch Shift Pro

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Pitch Shift Pro ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our extension handles information when you use it.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Pitch Shift Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or user data with external servers. All operations are performed locally on your device.

2. Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's local storage (localStorage) exclusively to save your audio presets, cuts, automations, and loop settings. This data remains on your computer and is never sent to us or any third parties. You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or using the delete functions within the extension.

3. Permissions

To function correctly, Pitch Shift Pro requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab and scripting: These are strictly used to access and modify the audio context of the active YouTube tab you are currently viewing, allowing the extension to change pitch and speed. The extension does not read or monitor your activity on any other tabs or websites.
  • Host Permission (*://*.youtube.com/*): Required to apply the audio effects specifically on YouTube video pages.

4. Third-Party Services

  • The extension interacts with YouTube to modify audio playback.
  • The "Original Key Search" feature simply opens a new tab with a standard Google Search query based on the video title. It does not track your searches.
  • Donation links direct you to external, secure payment processors (e.g., PayPal or LivePix). We do not collect or process any financial information.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Effective Date".
